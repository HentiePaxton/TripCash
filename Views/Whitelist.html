Home sharing platform

  • A decentralised online marketplace where people can lease or rent apartments, hotel rooms and any other accommodation.

Taxi Service

  • Includes a passenger app and a driver app similar to Uber. Safety checks are done with a partner firm.

Travel Marketplace

  • Decentralised marketplace for travel related goods and services such as tour packages, cruises, air tickets, bags and accessories. All participants will go through a screening process and be approved by arbiters and master node holders ensuring products are travel related.

Online Wallets

  • Online wallets are available for all major cryptocurrencies making the process of booking your next destination seamlessly easy.
